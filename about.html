About Call of Duty

Overview

Call of Duty is a popular first-person shooter video game series developed by Infinity Ward, Treyarch, and Sledgehammer Games. It was first released in 2003 and has since become one of the best-selling video game franchises of all time.

History

The first Call of Duty game was set during World War II, but subsequent installments have explored various time periods, including the Cold War, modern warfare, and futuristic settings. The series is known for its intense multiplayer mode, cinematic single-player campaigns, and addictive cooperative gameplay.

Legacy

Call of Duty has had a significant impact on the gaming industry, influencing other first-person shooter games and popularizing features such as killstreaks, customizable loadouts, and seasonal content updates. The franchise continues to evolve with new titles and expansions released regularly.
